Optional music goes in this folder. The game is fully playable without it
(all timing comes from the JavaScript clock). If you add these exact filenames
they will be played during each stage:

  song1.mp3   (Stage 1 - Subaru)
  song2.mp3   (Stage 2 - Angry Subaru)
  song3.mp3   (Stage 3 - Aura Monster Subaru)

Tip: the note charts in ../../charts/ are timed for ~48-56 second songs.
