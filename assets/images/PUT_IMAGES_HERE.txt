Optional character/trophy images go in this folder. The game works without
them (it draws shapes instead), but if you add these exact filenames they will
be used automatically:

  alcor_subaru.png         (the player)
  subaru.png               (Stage 1 boss)
  angry_subaru.png         (Stage 2 boss)
  aura_monster_subaru.png  (Stage 3 boss)
  trophy.png               (final win screen)

Recommended size: roughly 200 x 240 px, transparent PNG.
