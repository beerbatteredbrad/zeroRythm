How To Play

Notes fall toward the four lanes at the bottom. Press the matching arrow key when a note reaches its lane target:

◄ Left ▼ Down ▲ Up ► Right

Hit notes to damage the boss and build combo. Miss notes and you lose health. Survive the song to win the stage!
